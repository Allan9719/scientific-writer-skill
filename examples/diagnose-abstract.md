# DIAGNOSE Example: Abstract

## Prompt

Diagnose whether this Abstract follows the expected model:

> Macrophage activation contributes to persistent inflammation in rheumatoid arthritis. However, systemic glucocorticoid treatment can cause dose-limiting adverse effects. Here, we developed dexamethasone prodrug microspheres designed to improve macrophage-associated delivery. The microspheres were prepared by nanoprecipitation and characterized for size, morphology, and drug loading. In stimulated macrophage-like cells, microsphere treatment reduced inflammatory marker expression compared with free drug treatment. These findings suggest that prodrug microspheres may provide a useful platform for anti-inflammatory drug delivery.

## Expected Skill Behavior

The assistant should:

1. Map sentences to Abstract Components 1-9, then assess Component 10 globally.
2. Identify whether method, results, implications, achievement, applications, and flow are present.
3. Flag missing or weak components without inventing missing data.
4. Recommend specific additions only as placeholders when exact results are unavailable.
